About Us

URLBulkOpener

You know how annoying it gets when you have to open a dozen links one after another? We felt the same problem — that's exactly why we designed the url bulk opener.

It's a fast and simple tool made to save time for people who work with multiple URLs every day — whether it's for SEO, research, making backlinks or just browsing.

We didn't want it to feel complicated or technical. So there's no sign-up, no setup, and no waiting. Just paste your links, click once, and everything opens instantly. That's it.

We created it for anyone who deals with thousands of links daily — SEO experts, developers, marketers, researchers, or even everyday users who value speed and simplicity. No sign-ups, no extensions and no hidden charges its totally free for anyone — just clean, fast, and reliable performance straight from your browser.

Key Features

Customizable Time Delays – Control how fast your URLs open.
Flexible Tab Management – Choose whether to open links in new tabs, new windows, or reuse existing ones.
Local Storage Option – Save your frequently used URL lists right in your browser for next time.
Privacy First – No sign-ups, no accounts, no tracking. Everything stays securely on your device.
Fast and Lightweight – Designed for smooth performance, no matter how many URLs you open.
